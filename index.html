Who We Are

A Global Group Built on Vision & Integrity

Artha Management and Holdings Inc is the apex holding entity of the Artha Group, overseeing and managing a portfolio of wholly-owned subsidiaries across the United States and India. Headquartered in New Jersey, the group was founded with a clear mandate: to build sustainable, long-term value across sectors that matter.

Through its US-registered subsidiary, Artha Management and Holdings Overseas, the group controls Artha Management and Holdings India Pvt Ltd — the Indian holding company — which in turn fully owns and operates Artha Property Management, Artha Farms, and Artha Capital Management.

  • ✓ Structured for cross-border governance and compliance
  • ✓ Long-term capital allocation across high-growth sectors
  • ✓ Leadership with deep expertise in US and Indian markets
